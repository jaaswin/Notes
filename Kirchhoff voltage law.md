Kirchhoff‚Äôs Voltage Law states that in any closed loop of an electrical circuit, the algebraic sum of all voltages is zero.

Simply put:
Total voltage rises = Total voltage drops

Physical Principle Behind KVL

KVL is based on the law of conservation of energy.
Energy supplied by sources is completely used by circuit elements like resistors, capacitors, and inductors.
